Welcome to Simulation Magic!

Simulation Magic products are designed to provide users with the ability to simulate a variety of processes, systems and scenarios. These simulations are based on the principle that mathematical algorithms and engineering models form the basis for all computer simulations of real systems.

From risk mitigation to industrial processes to physical systems, we implement models to help people simulate reality, in order to provide support for their business and personal decisions.

The initial offerings in this series of products are the Personal Finance Magic and Small Business Magic products, which support financial planning for individuals and businesses. Users can track their personal or business net worth over time under variable conditions corresponding to various simulations, through forecasting and scenario analysis.

A comprehensive picture of depreciation/appreciation and net present value is presented to manage assets, support decisions and mitigate risk. Costs are determined for the acquisition and/or maintenance of vehicles, buildings, equipment, etc. The result is a comprehensive analysis that is suitable for financial planning by individuals, families or businesses.

To get started on your project, select the 'Working on Simulations' item from the menu at the top of the page.
