How to Display Reports

This page is the starting point for choosing which reports to display. Since the configufation details for the visualization are entered in the graphical parameters tab of the 'Working on Simulations' page, the tabs of the 'How to display reports' page determine what aspect of the visualization will be displayed.

For example, you can choose to view the operating costs, net worth, or risk analysis. For more detailed explanationsof the various types of Visualizations available to you, please see the system's Help screens.

To view the results of descriptive, predictive or prescriptive Analytics, select the 'Display Analytics' item from the menu at the top of the page. .

Descriptin of Tab Selections for this Page


Tab Description
How to display reports This tab provides an entry point for displaying the reports of your analytics and sinulations
Operating costs This tab displays the operating costs of your personal or business project
Net worth This tab allows you to visualize the net worth of your projectas a funcion of expenses, reveneues, assets, etc.
Descriptive results Here is wher you can view the current state of the system that you're analyzing.
Predictive results This tab shows you what your system will probabaly look like in the future, based on the analysis.
Prescriptive recommendations This is where the program advises you on how you can mitigate risks and improve the expected outcome for your system.
